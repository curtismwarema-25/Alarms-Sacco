Short-Term & Educational Loans

Quick solutions for urgent needs and dedicated support for educational pursuits.

Emergency Loans

Designed to assist members in critical or urgent financial obligations with fast approval.

  • Purpose: Critical or urgent financial needs.
  • Interest Rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment Period: 12 months.
  • Requirements:
    • Current payslip
    • Three (3) guarantors
    • One (1) supporting document
    • One (1) witness

School Fee Loans

Processed for members to meet school fees requirements for their children, ensuring uninterrupted education.

  • Purpose: Meeting school fees requirements.
  • Interest Rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment Period: 12 months.
  • Requirements:
    • Current payslip
    • School fee structure
    • Adequate guarantors
  • Processing: Available from January to March every year.

University / College Loans

Specifically designed for members to cover college and university fees for themselves or their children.

  • Purpose: Covering college/university fees.
  • Interest Rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment Period: Up to 2 years.
  • Requirements:
    • Current payslip
    • Copies of guarantors ID Cards
    • Joining instructions from the college/university
    • Five (5) guarantors

Development & Quick Access Loans

Fuel your long-term growth projects or get immediate funds for urgent needs.

Normal / Development Loans

Aids members in long-term income-generating assets and activities, fostering sustainable financial growth.

  • Purpose: Long-term income-generating assets/activities.
  • Interest Rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment Period: 48 months.
  • Requirements:
    • Current payslip
    • Five (5) guarantors
    • Copy of guarantors National Identification Cards
    • One (1) witness
    • Phone numbers of guarantors

Instant Loans

A short-term loan for immediate day-to-day issues and rent payments, with quick processing and recovery in the same month.

  • Purpose: Day-to-day issues, rent payments.
  • Interest Rate: 1.5% straight line.
  • Repayment: Recoverable in the same month.

Special & Asset Acquisition Loans

Loans to assist with occasional urgent needs or significant asset purchases.

Special / Item Loans

Processed to assist members meet their urgent, occasional needs, or acquire specific items. Quotations for items are required.

  • Purpose: Urgent needs, occasional items.
  • Interest Rate: 2.0% straight line.
  • Repayment Periods: 3 months and 6 months respectively.
  • Requirements: Written quotations for items.

Motorbike, Tuk-Tuk, Vehicle & Other Item Loans

Specialized loans for acquiring income-generating assets like motorbikes, tuk-tuks, vehicles, or other business items.

  • Purpose: Acquiring specific income-generating assets.
  • Interest Rate: 2.0% straight line.
  • Repayment Period: 30 months.

Repurchase / Top-up Loans

These loans are for members who need to complete a project that was started but not yet finished, allowing for additional funding by restructuring existing loans.

  • Purpose: Complete unfinished projects or restructure existing loans.
  • Interest Rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment Period: 48 months.
