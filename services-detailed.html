Our Comprehensive Services

Savings & Deposits

Build your financial foundation with our secure and flexible savings solutions, tailored for both long-term goals and short-term needs.

Member Deposits & Shares (Long Term)

This forms the core of your membership and financial stake in the SACCO. Contributions to your long-term savings and shares are non-withdrawable, ensuring sustained growth and providing the base for accessing other SACCO benefits.

  • Non-withdrawable contributions.
  • Forms basis for loan eligibility.
  • Contributes to SACCO's capital.

W.S.S. Savings (Short Term)

Our Withdrawal Savings Scheme (W.S.S.) offers a flexible way to save for planned annual expenses. Make monthly contributions for 11 months, with full withdrawal available every December.

  • Monthly saving of KES 500.
  • Withdrawal available every December.
  • Ideal for holiday or end-year expenses.

Loans & Credit Facilities

Access a variety of loans designed to meet your diverse needs, whether for personal development, education, business expansion, or emergencies.

Emergency Loans

Designed to assist members in critical or urgent financial obligations. Get fast approval to cover immediate needs.

  • Interest rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Requirements: Current payslip, 3 guarantors, 1 supporting document, 1 witness.
  • Repayment period: 12 months.
Apply Now

School Fees Loans

Processed for members to meet school fees requirements for their children. Ensure uninterrupted education.

  • Interest rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Requirements: Current payslip.
  • School fee structure required.
  • Repayment period: 12 months.
  • Adequate guarantors required.
Apply Now

University / College Loans

Specifically for members to meet college and university fees for themselves or their children.

  • Interest rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment period: Up to 2 years.
  • Requirements: Current payslip, copies of guarantors ID cards, joining instructions from college/university, 5 guarantors.
Apply Now

Normal / Development Loans

Assists members in long-term income generating assets and activities, fostering sustainable financial growth.

  • Interest rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment period: 48 months.
  • Requirements: Current payslip, 5 guarantors, copy of guarantors national ID, 1 witness, phone numbers of guarantors.
Apply Now

Instant Loans

A short-term loan for immediate day-to-day issues and rent payments, with quick processing and recovery.

  • Interest rate: 1.5% straight line.
  • Repayment and recovery in the same month.
Apply Now

Special / Item Loans

Processed to help members meet urgent, occasional needs or acquire specific items. Quotations for items are required.

  • Interest rate: 2.0% straight line.
  • Repayment periods: 3 or 6 months.
Apply Now

Repurchase / Top-up Loans

These loans are for members who need to complete a project that was started but not yet finished, allowing for additional funding.

  • Interest rate: 1.25% on reducing balance.
  • Repayment period: 48 months.

Motorbike, Tuk-Tuk, Vehicle & Other Item Loans

Specialized loans for acquiring income-generating assets like motorbikes, tuk-tuks, vehicles, or other business items.
