About ALARMS SACCO

ALARMS NON-NWDT SACCO LTD is a member-driven cooperative society originally founded to serve security guards and factory personnel. What began as a focused financial solution for a specific sector has since grown into a dynamic and inclusive SACCO, open to members from various industries. With a strong foundation in cooperative values and financial empowerment, ALARMS SACCO continues to support its members through savings, credit facilities, and financial education. The society remains firmly grounded in its goal to enhance the welfare and economic prosperity of all members.

Our Journey & Commitment

ALARM SACCO has grown into a thriving institution, committed to providing accessible and beneficial financial solutions to our members.

Alarms NON-NWDT SACCO LTD traces its roots back to 1990, when it was first established to serve members from security and guard companies. Officially registered as a SACCO under the Co-operative Societies Act in 1994, it was born out of the need to provide a platform for financial empowerment, particularly for factory and security guards. Over time, our commitment to inclusivity and growth led us to expand our bond to include members from a wider catchment area such as Export Processing Zones (EPZs), contracted companies, and other security service providers. This evolution reflects our unwavering commitment to promoting the economic well-being and welfare of our members, regardless of their background or employer.

Our Comprehensive Services

Designed to empower your financial journey, our services offer flexibility, security, and growth opportunities for every member.

Solutions Tailored for You

Explore our range of financial products and services, crafted to support your personal and business aspirations as a valued member.

Member Deposits & Shares

Build your financial stake in the SACCO with regular deposits and share contributions. These form the foundation of your membership and provide access to our full range of benefits.

Development Loans

Access a variety of loans designed to help you achieve your personal and business goals, such as education, home improvement, business expansion, or asset acquisition. Enjoy competitive interest rates and flexible repayment terms.

FOSA Savings Accounts

Benefit from convenient Front Office Service Activity (FOSA) accounts for easy deposits, withdrawals, and mobile banking. Manage your daily finances with the security and accessibility of a SACCO.

Investment & Welfare Funds

Participate in specialized funds designed for long-term growth and member welfare. These include long-term investment schemes and welfare benefits like benevolent funds.

Guaranteed Loans (BOSA)

Utilize our Back Office Service Activity (BOSA) for secured loans, often guaranteed by member deposits or fellow members, allowing for larger loan amounts based on your savings and guarantees.

Financial Advisory

Receive personalized guidance on managing your finances, saving strategies, and investment opportunities within the SACCO, helping you make informed decisions for your financial well-being.
